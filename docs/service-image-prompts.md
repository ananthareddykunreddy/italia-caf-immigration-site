# Service Image Prompt Pack

Use these prompts to generate the remaining service images in the same style as the uploaded CAF Bixio examples.

Style rules for all prompts:
- clean professional Italian service-poster illustration
- square 1:1 image
- white or very light neutral background
- realistic paperwork on a desk
- soft commercial illustration look
- readable Italian service title at the top
- include related objects only when relevant
- polished office lighting
- no watermarks
- no extra random text except the service title and, if useful, a short official-looking subtitle
